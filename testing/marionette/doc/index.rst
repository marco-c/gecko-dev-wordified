Marionette
is
an
automation
driver
for
Mozilla
s
Gecko
engine
.
It
can
remotely
control
either
the
UI
or
the
internal
JavaScript
of
Gecko
-
based
browsers
such
as
Firefox
and
Fennec
.
It
can
control
both
the
chrome
and
the
content
document
giving
a
high
level
of
control
and
ability
to
replicate
user
interaction
.
In
addition
to
performing
actions
on
the
browser
Marionette
can
also
ready
properties
and
attributes
of
the
DOM
.
